## Description

I built a local AI Personal OS: one folder on my laptop that gives Claude context about my life, business, projects, decisions, goals, tools, and workflows.

Most people use AI like a vending machine. They open a chat, ask a question, get an answer, close it, and repeat the same explanation tomorrow.

This video shows the fix: give Claude a home.

One folder. One brain file. Your context, projects, decisions, references, templates, and skills in one place.

When you do that, AI stops being a random chatbot and starts becoming an assistant that compounds.
